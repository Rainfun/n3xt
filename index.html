Terms of Service

1. Acceptance

By accessing N3XT you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, do not use the platform.

2. Content

N3XT indexes music metadata and provides links to publicly available audio previews. We do not host copyrighted audio files on our servers.

3. Leaked Content

Some content on N3XT originates from community-sourced channels. Upon receiving valid DMCA notices we remove content promptly and fully.

4. Limitation of Liability

N3XT is provided as-is. We make no guarantees about availability or accuracy of content.

5. Contact

For takedown requests use the DMCA form. For other inquiries contact us through official channels.
